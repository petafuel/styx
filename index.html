The vision of Styx

The Revised Payment Services Directive (PSD2) is intended to harmonise digital payment transactions within the European Economic Area. In addition to banks, PSD2 also provides many opportunities for third-party service providers (TTPs) to offer innovative services through the payment services provided by banks. Although PSD2 is based on a standard for bank interfaces, there remains considerable scope for implementation. For TTPs, developing and maintaining an XS2A interface is a time-consuming challenge. With the PSD2 XS2A Client Styx we offer TTPs a free and easy solution to interact with the different PSD2 interfaces of banks from Germany and Europe.

Styx Features

The XS2A Client Styx supports the following services:

Payment Initiation Service

Initiation of electronic payment transactions

Payment Instrument Issuing Service

Check if sufficient funds are available on payment account

Account Information Service

Provision of account information of the payment account

Strong Customer Authentication

Consent Service for various multi-factor authentication (MFA) methods

Use Cases for Styx

Multibanking made simple.

Various multibanking tools such as payment instigations or accessing account information can be implemented by the XS2A Client Styx.

A depositer (PSU) is for example able to use a TPP App to initiate a payment. The TPP App interfaces with the Styx CM to receive approval from the PSU through a SCA method. After having received approval the Styx PIS-Client is opened via the Styx API to access the XS2A API of the bank in charge of the account to initiate the payment.
